Hunting

My Favorite Hobby

I've Had a love for hunting and the outdoors as long as I can remember. It was difficult in the beginning
because nobody else in my family shared the same hobby. As I grew and learned it turned into a real passion of mine.
I taught myself and became very skilled in the sport. In my time hunting, I've hunted various types of wild game in
several states, but mostly North Carolina and Utah. In between hunting seasons I spend my time hiking to gather deer and elk
sheds, and hunting open season game such as Coyotes.

  1. North Carolina Game I've Hunted
    • Whitetail Deer
    • Turkey
    • Dove
    • Coyote
  2. Utah Game I've Hunted
    • Mule deer
    • Elk
    • Coyote
